How It Works

Step 1: Browse Listings

Easily explore a variety of local businesses available for sale. Each listing provides essential information such as revenue, industry, years in operation, location, and the seller’s motivation for selling.

Step 2: Sign NDA

To access detailed financial documents, simply sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) directly on our platform. This ensures confidentiality and transparency between both parties.

Step 3: Communicate & Offer

Engage directly with sellers through our secure chat interface. Discuss the details, ask questions, and when ready, submit your offer directly through the platform.

Key Features

For Sellers

  • Listing Creation: Easily list your business with a step-by-step process.
  • Document Control: Choose to automatically share your business's financials with potential buyers post-NDA, or manually approve each request.
  • Seller Dashboard: Monitor your listings, track offers, and manage communications all in one place.

For Buyers

  • NDA & LOI Signing: Sign NDAs and Letters of Intent (LOIs) digitally to unlock detailed business information and submit your formal offer.
  • Buyer Dashboard: Keep track of businesses you’ve viewed, NDAs you’ve signed, and offers you’ve made.
  • Direct Communication: Engage in direct, secure conversations with sellers through our in-app messaging system.
